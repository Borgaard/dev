Real Analysis in a Nutshell
===

A sequence of numbers $$( x_0, x_1, x_2, \dots )$$ fits the
*Cauchy cirterion* if:

$$
\forall n, \\
\exists m, \\
s.t. |x_j - x_k| \le \frac{1}{n} \\
if j,k \ge m
$$

If a sequence of numbers fits the Cauchy criterion, it is
a *Cauchy sequence*.
