Sum of Square Roots Notes
===


The sum of square roots problem is:

$$
\begin{align}
 n, m \in \mathbb{Z} \\
 a_j, b_k \in \mathbb{Z} \\
 \sum_j^{n-1} \sqrt a_j =? \sum_k^{m-1} \sqrt b_k
\end{align}
$$

In other words, is checking the sum of square roots in NP?
